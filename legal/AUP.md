# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** _______________

This Acceptable Use Policy applies to all use of the Lecsy Services. By using the Services, Customer and its Authorized Users agree to comply with this AUP.

## Prohibited Activities

Customer and its Authorized Users may not:

### 1. Illegal or Harmful Use
- Use the Services for any illegal purpose or in violation of any applicable law or regulation
- Record any individual without obtaining all necessary consents under applicable wiretapping, privacy, and education laws (including FERPA, GDPR, COPPA where applicable)
- Use the Services to harass, abuse, defame, or threaten any individual

### 2. Security Violations
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Services, other accounts, or any networks or systems connected to the Services
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Services without prior written permission
- Bypass any access controls, rate limits, or authentication mechanisms
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any portion of the Services

### 3. Service Abuse
- Transmit malware, viruses, or other harmful code
- Engage in any activity that interferes with or disrupts the Services
- Use automated scripts to access the Services in a manner that exceeds normal usage patterns
- Resell, sublicense, or redistribute the Services without prior written authorization

### 4. Content Violations
- Upload, transmit, or store content that is unlawful, infringing, defamatory, obscene, or otherwise objectionable
- Use the Services to infringe any intellectual property right
- Use the Services to collect or harvest personal data of others without consent

### 5. AI / Machine Learning Misuse
- Use AI features to generate content that could harm individuals, including misinformation, deepfakes, or impersonation
- Use the Services to train competing machine learning models
- Submit prompts designed to circumvent the AI's safety measures

## Educational Recording Compliance

Customers using the Services in educational settings must:
- Obtain consent from students (and parents/guardians for minors) before recording
- Comply with FERPA when handling student records
- Provide appropriate notices about how recordings will be used and stored
- Honor student rights to access, correct, and delete their data

## Reporting Violations

Suspected violations of this AUP should be reported to `abuse@lecsy.app`.

## Enforcement

Provider may, in its sole discretion, suspend or terminate access to the Services for any violation of this AUP. For severe or repeated violations, Provider may terminate the underlying agreement immediately upon written notice.

## Updates

Provider may update this AUP from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to organization owners at least 30 days in advance.
