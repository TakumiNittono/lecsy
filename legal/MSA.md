# Master Services Agreement (MSA)

**Version:** 1.0 (TEMPLATE — pending counsel review)
**Effective Date:** _______________

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Lecsy** ("Provider"), and **[Customer Legal Name]** ("Customer").

## 1. Definitions

- **"Services"** means the Lecsy software-as-a-service platform, including the iOS application and web administration console, and any related documentation.
- **"Authorized Users"** means individuals authorized by Customer to access the Services as members of Customer's organization.
- **"Customer Data"** means data, content, or information submitted to the Services by Customer or its Authorized Users.
- **"Order Form"** means a written or electronic ordering document that references this Agreement.
- **"Subscription Term"** means the period during which Customer is authorized to access the Services as set forth in an Order Form.

## 2. Services

2.1 Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the Services during the Subscription Term solely for Customer's internal educational or business purposes.

2.2 Provider will provide the Services in accordance with the SLA attached as Exhibit A.

2.3 Provider may update the Services from time to time, provided no update materially reduces functionality.

## 3. Customer Responsibilities

3.1 Customer is responsible for: (a) all activities of its Authorized Users, (b) the accuracy and legality of Customer Data, (c) obtaining all consents required for the recording and processing of audio (including, where applicable, FERPA-compliant consents from students or guardians), and (d) complying with all applicable laws.

3.2 Customer shall not: (a) reverse engineer the Services, (b) use the Services to transmit harmful code, (c) attempt to gain unauthorized access, (d) use the Services in violation of the Acceptable Use Policy.

## 4. Fees and Payment

4.1 Customer shall pay the fees set forth in the Order Form. Fees are stated in U.S. dollars and are non-refundable except as expressly provided.

4.2 Invoices are due NET 30 unless otherwise stated. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum permitted by law, whichever is less.

4.3 Provider may suspend Services for accounts more than 30 days past due, after providing 10 days' written notice.

4.4 Fees exclude all taxes. Customer is responsible for all applicable taxes other than taxes on Provider's net income.

## 5. Term and Termination

5.1 This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.

5.2 Subscription Terms are set forth in Order Forms and auto-renew for successive equal terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days before the end of the then-current term.

5.3 Either party may terminate for material breach not cured within 30 days of written notice.

5.4 Upon termination: (a) Customer's access to the Services ceases, (b) Provider will make Customer Data available for export for 30 days, (c) thereafter Provider will delete Customer Data in accordance with its Data Retention Policy.

## 6. Confidentiality

Each party will protect the other's Confidential Information using the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, but not less than reasonable care. Confidential Information may be used only to perform under this Agreement.

## 7. Data Protection and Security

7.1 The Data Processing Agreement (DPA) attached as Exhibit B is incorporated by reference where applicable.

7.2 Provider implements administrative, physical, and technical safeguards designed to protect Customer Data, as described in Exhibit C (Security Overview).

7.3 Provider will notify Customer of any confirmed Security Incident affecting Customer Data without undue delay and in any case within 72 hours.

## 8. Intellectual Property

8.1 Customer retains all rights in Customer Data.

8.2 Provider retains all rights in the Services.

8.3 Customer grants Provider a limited license to use Customer Data solely to provide the Services and in aggregated, de-identified form to improve the Services.

## 9. Warranties and Disclaimers

9.1 Each party represents that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement.

9.2 Provider warrants that the Services will perform materially in accordance with the documentation.

9.3 EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED, THE SERVICES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS." PROVIDER DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED.

## 10. Limitation of Liability

10.1 NEITHER PARTY'S AGGREGATE LIABILITY EXCEEDS THE FEES PAID BY CUSTOMER IN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO LIABILITY.

10.2 NEITHER PARTY IS LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES.

10.3 The above limitations do not apply to: (a) breaches of confidentiality, (b) indemnification obligations, (c) Customer's payment obligations, (d) gross negligence or willful misconduct.

## 11. Indemnification

11.1 Provider will defend and indemnify Customer against third-party claims that the Services infringe intellectual property rights, except to the extent caused by Customer Data or Customer's modifications.

11.2 Customer will defend and indemnify Provider against third-party claims arising from Customer Data or Customer's misuse of the Services.

## 12. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, U.S.A., without regard to conflict of laws principles. Any disputes shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in Delaware.

## 13. Miscellaneous

13.1 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, including its Exhibits and Order Forms, is the entire agreement between the parties.

13.2 **Amendments.** No amendment is effective unless in writing signed by both parties.

13.3 **Assignment.** Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other's consent, except in connection with a merger or sale of substantially all assets.

13.4 **Force Majeure.** Neither party is liable for delays caused by events beyond its reasonable control.

13.5 **Notices.** Notices must be in writing and sent to the addresses specified in the Order Form.
